Bio

Zhaopeng Tu is a principal researcher at Tencent AI Lab, whose research focuses on deep learning for natural language processing (NLP). He is currently working on neural machine translation (NMT) and Seq2Seq learning for other NLP tasks, such as dialogue and question answering. He has published over 50 papers in leading NLP/AI journals and conferences such as ACL, EMNLP, TACL, AAAI, and IJCAI. He served as Associate Editor of NeuroComputing, Area Chair of ACL2019, EMNLP2018-2019, NAACL2019, and Senior PC Member of AAAI2019, IJCAI2021.
